Big Bears

The Big Bear class is the morning session for 4- and 5-year old children. Classroom activities build on previous learning experiences, and children’s interests help drive projects and collaborative play. Teachers support each child’s readiness for kindergarten across all academic, social and emotional areas.

Classroom Goals

The nursery school ensures that our school environment is safe, healthy and conducive to children’s exploration and independence. Our teachers encourage students to:

  • Support a positive self image.
  • Develop social competence.
  • Encourage independence.
  • Foster a positive attitude towards school and learning.
  • Provide opportunities for cognitive, social, aesthetic and physical growth.
  • Create a comfortable environment for exploring and embracing diversity.
  • Work cooperatively with each family.
